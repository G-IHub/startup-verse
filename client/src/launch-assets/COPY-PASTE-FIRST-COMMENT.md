## 📝 WRITING TIPS

### **Do:**

✅ Be authentic (tell your real story)  
✅ Use emojis (sparingly - 1-2 per section)  
✅ Break into sections (headers with \*\*)  
✅ Include specific numbers (builds credibility)  
✅ Make it scannable (bullets, short paragraphs)  
✅ End with clear CTA (link + offer)  
✅ Offer to answer questions (be available)

### **Don't:**

❌ Copy others' first comments verbatim  
❌ Over-promise ("We're better than Slack/Notion combined!")  
❌ Be salesy ("BUY NOW! LIMITED TIME!")  
❌ Write walls of text (break it up)  
❌ Forget your link (seems obvious, but people do it)  
❌ Ignore negative comments (engage with everyone)
