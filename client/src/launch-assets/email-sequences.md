# Email Best Practices

### **Subject Lines:**

✅ Keep under 50 characters  
✅ Use emojis sparingly (1 per subject)  
✅ Avoid spam words ("FREE!", "ACT NOW!")  
✅ A/B test different angles

### **Preview Text:**

✅ 40-130 characters  
✅ Complements subject (not repeats)  
✅ Creates urgency or curiosity

### **Body:**

✅ Write like a human (not a corporation)  
✅ Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)  
✅ Use bullets for scanability  
✅ One clear CTA per email  
✅ Sign with your real name

### **CTAs:**

✅ Use buttons (not just links)  
✅ Action-oriented ("Get Started", not "Click Here")  
✅ One primary CTA, max 2 secondary

### **Send Times:**

✅ Weekdays > weekends  
✅ Tuesday-Thursday = best open rates  
✅ 9-11 AM or 1-3 PM (recipient timezone)  
✅ Avoid Monday mornings (inbox overload)

# Metrics to Track

**Per Email:**

- Open rate (aim for 20-30%)
- Click-through rate (aim for 2-5%)
- Conversion rate (depends on goal)
- Unsubscribe rate (< 0.5% is good)

**Overall:**

- List growth rate
- Engagement over time (are opens declining?)
- Revenue per email sent
- Most engaging email type
