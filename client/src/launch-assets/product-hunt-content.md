### **Q: "How is this different from Notion/Slack/Linear?"**

```
Great question!

The key difference is we're built SPECIFICALLY for early-stage startups:

1. **All-in-One:** Instead of paying $30-50/month for 3-5 tools, you get everything in one workspace.

2. **Smart Team Matching:** None of those tools help you find co-founders or early talent. That's our killer feature.

3. **Startup Journey Tracking:** We guide you from ideation → MVP → launch → growth with stage-based recommendations.

4. **Simpler:** Notion is incredibly powerful but overwhelming. We're focused on what you need in the first 0-12 months.

5. **Free for Small Teams:** Notion, Slack, Linear all charge per user. We're free up to 5.

Think of us as "Slack + Linear + AngelList, but simplified for the pre-seed to Series A stage."

Does that help?
```

```
Freemium:

**Free Tier** (forever):
- Up to 5 team members
- All core features
- Limited Team Matching (5 matches/month)

**Pro Tier** ($29/month):
- Unlimited team members
- Unlimited Team Matching
- Video call recording
- Priority support
- Analytics

**Growth Tier** ($99/month):
- Multiple startups/projects
- API access
- Custom workflows
- Dedicated success manager

Our goal: Be free for as long as possible while you're bootstrapping, then grow with you.
```

### **Q: "How do you make money if it's free for teams up to 5?"**

```
Good question! Here's the model:

1. **Free users → word of mouth:** They tell other founders, we grow.

2. **Teams scale:** When you go from 5 → 10 people, you upgrade to Pro ($29/mo).

3. **Premium features:** Unlimited Team Matching, analytics, API access are Pro-only.

4. **Multiple startups:** Serial founders managing 2-3 projects need Growth tier.

5. **Partnerships:** We're exploring partnerships with accelerators (YC, Techstars).

Also, we've found that founders LOVE paying for tools that actually help them succeed. If we save you $2,700/year vs. buying Slack + Notion + Linear, $29/month feels like a steal.

Make sense?
```
