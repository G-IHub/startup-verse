## 💡 Pro Tips from Successful Launches

### **What Works:**

✅ **Authentic first comment** - Tell your story, don't just list features  
✅ **Respond to everyone** - Every comment, every question  
✅ **Build in public** - Share your journey for 2-4 weeks before launch  
✅ **Pre-build waitlist** - 500+ waitlist = strong launch day  
✅ **Demo video** - Products with videos get 3x more upvotes  
✅ **Engage others** - Comment on other launches, be supportive

### **What Doesn't Work:**

❌ **Generic responses** - Copy/paste replies feel inauthentic  
❌ **Only promoting** - Give value, don't just ask for upvotes  
❌ **Ignoring negative feedback** - Embrace criticism, learn from it  
❌ **Launching on Friday** - Tuesday-Thursday are best  
❌ **No video** - Visuals are essential  
❌ **Radio silence after launch** - Keep momentum going

## 🆘 Need Help?

### **Common Questions:**

**Q: When should I launch?**  
A: Tuesday-Thursday, 4-6 weeks from now. Avoid holidays and major events.

**Q: What if I don't have 500 waitlist signups?**  
A: You can still launch! Just build your network in advance (friends, family, Twitter).

**Q: Do I need a video?**  
A: Highly recommended. Products with videos get 2-3x more engagement.

**Q: What's the most important thing?**  
A: Your first comment. Post within 60 seconds and tell your authentic story.

**Q: How do I get to #1 Product of the Day?**  
A: Strong waitlist + authentic engagement + great product + luck. Aim for top 5.

**Q: What if something goes wrong?**  
A: Stay calm. Communicate transparently. Fix issues fast. People are forgiving.
