## Production Tips

### **Screen Recording:**

- **Tool:** Loom, ScreenFlow, or Camtasia
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps or 60fps
- **Hide:** Desktop icons, notifications, bookmarks bar
- **Cursor:** Make it larger and add click animations

### **Mouse Movement:**

- Move slowly and deliberately
- Pause for 1-2 seconds on important elements
- Use smooth, curved movements (not jerky)

### **Transitions:**

- Fade (0.5 seconds)
- Slide (for panel animations)
- No cheesy effects

### **Music:**

**Recommended Tracks** (Epidemic Sound / Artlist / YouTube Audio Library):

- "Upbeat Corporate" vibes
- "Tech Startup" category
- 120-130 BPM
- No lyrics

**Suggested:**

- "Inspire" by Corporate
- "Innovation" by Technology
- "Startup Energy" (search on Epidemic Sound)

### **Voiceover:**

**If recording yourself:**

- Use good mic (Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB)
- Quiet room (closet works well for sound dampening)
- Speak slowly and clearly
- Smile while talking (it comes through in your voice)
- Do 3-5 takes, pick the best

**If hiring:**

- Fiverr: $50-100 for professional VO
- Voice123: $100-200 for higher quality
- Provide this script + tone: "Professional but friendly, energetic but not salesy"

### **Text Overlays (if no voiceover):**

- Font: Inter, Roboto, or SF Pro (clean, modern)
- Size: Large enough to read on mobile
- Color: White text with dark shadow, or vice versa
- Animation: Fade in, stay 2-3 seconds, fade out
- Position: Center or lower third

## Editing Checklist

### **Video:**

- [ ] Intro: Logo + tagline (2-3 seconds)
- [ ] Problem statement (8-10 seconds)
- [ ] Feature demonstrations (40-50 seconds)
- [ ] Pricing + CTA (10-15 seconds)
- [ ] End card with URL (3-5 seconds)

### **Audio:**

- [ ] Voiceover OR text overlays
- [ ] Background music (30-50% volume, don't overpower voice)
- [ ] Sound effects (optional: subtle UI clicks, whooshes for transitions)
- [ ] Audio levels normalized
- [ ] No clipping or distortion

### **Polish:**

- [ ] Color grading (consistent look)
- [ ] Brightness/contrast adjusted
- [ ] Cursor visible and smooth
- [ ] No typos in text overlays
- [ ] No dead air (music throughout)
- [ ] Export in 1080p (Full HD)

## Final Checklist

**Before Recording:**

- [ ] Clean up browser (close tabs, hide bookmarks)
- [ ] Set display resolution to 1920x1080
- [ ] Turn off notifications (Do Not Disturb)
- [ ] Prepare demo data (realistic tasks, messages, team members)
- [ ] Test screen recorder (audio + video working)
- [ ] Write shot list (what to show, in what order)

**During Recording:**

- [ ] Move cursor slowly
- [ ] Pause on important elements (2 seconds)
- [ ] Record 3-5 takes (pick the best)
- [ ] Record extra footage (B-roll for cutaways)

**After Recording:**

- [ ] Edit in timeline (cut dead space)
- [ ] Add voiceover OR text overlays
- [ ] Add background music (30-50% volume)
- [ ] Add transitions (subtle)
- [ ] Color grade (make it pop)
- [ ] Export in 1080p MP4

**Before Uploading:**

- [ ] Watch on mobile (is text readable?)
- [ ] Watch without sound (do you understand it?)
- [ ] Get feedback from 2-3 people
- [ ] Make final tweaks
- [ ] Upload to YouTube (public)
- [ ] Copy YouTube URL for Product Hunt
